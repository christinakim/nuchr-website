Get Involved

Northwestern students are welcome to apply for general membership at the start of each academic year. The application process consists of a short form and follow up interview. We are looking for students who are excited about human rights and social justice issues, regardless of major or academic background.

To apply to become a member of NUCHR, you simply have to fill out this form with your information. Applications for the 2014-2015 school year open Sunday September 28th and are due by Sunday, October 5th at 11:59pm. Interviews will take place on a rolling basis so submit your application early!

If you missed the application period or have any questions, feel free to contact us at conferenceonhumanrights@u.northwestern.edu.
