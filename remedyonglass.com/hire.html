NOW HIRING: FULL STACK DEVELOPERS

Remedy is an early-stage start-up helping doctors collaborate more meaningfully and more often. We use Google Glass to allow the provider on scene to show the patient to a specialist at a distance. Remedy is effective because it fits right into existing workflows without interrupting the clinicians normal line of work. With Remedy, patients get their concerns resolved sooner, and specialists see more patients. We’re live at three Harvard hospitals and plan to expand to three other institutions over the next quarter. We need you to make that happen.

What's the position like?
You will work with a small early stage team to build the future of Remedy. If you enjoy building innovative solutions that last, this is the place for you. As a member of this team, you will touch every part of the system - Glass, Multimedia, Django, Javascript/CSS, and more. Experience with Google Glass is not required, but an appetite to always learn new things is.

We’re looking for
· 1+ year(s) of development experience with Android
· 3+ years of software development experience
· Experience working with Python, Django, Java, Android, Javascript, CSS
· Broad experience, quick learner, people describe you as a “generalist”
· Ability to work full time in SF/Bay Area.
· A GitHub account with contributions
· Experience with video/audio capture and streaming
· Prior Android work that involved performance profiling
· Experience working with the NDK
· An ability to analyse UX/UI workflows for quality/efficiency

Cultural Fit
We’re all here because because we want to move mountains. Join us if you want to do the best work of your life. Join us if you want to build things that tangibly improves peoples lives. Join us if you're indefatigable.
