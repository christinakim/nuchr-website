Events

NUCHR sponsors a variety of forums on different human rights issues throughout the year, in addition to hosting our national conference in January. Events listed below are open to the public, as are a number of the conference panels later in the year.

If you are interested in co-sponsoring an event with NUCHR, please contact our Programming Coordinators: Kelby Schuetz kelbyschuetz2015@u.northwestern.edu, Jackson Walker jacksonwalker2017@u.northwestern.edu, and Isabel Roberston isabelrobertson2017@u.northwestern.edu
